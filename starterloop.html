FAQs
Is the loop flat?

Yes, no steep climbs. It's designed to be an easy to follow beginner-friendly route.

How long will it take?

About 7-10 minutes jogging or 12-15 minutes for walking for most people.

Is it safe at night?

Choose well-lit hours. This loop prioritizes open visibility at all times.

Can i repeat the loop?

Absolutely. Loop it as many times as you like to build distance gradually.
