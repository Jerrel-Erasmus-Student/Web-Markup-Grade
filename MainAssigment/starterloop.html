Quick Overview

  • Start at the park entrance.
  • Follow the main loop clockwise.
  • Cross two straight sections, no turns.
  • Finish where you started, repeat possible.

Tips

  • Warm up 3-5 minutes of walking.
  • Keep an easy pace; should be able to talk.
  • Hydrate well during and after.
  • Cool donw 3-5 minutes of walking.

FAQs

Is the loop flat?

Yes, no steep climbs. It's designed to be an easy to follow beginner-friendly route.

How long will it take?

About 7-10 minutes jogging or 12-15 minutes for walking for most poeple.

Is it safe at night?

Choose well-lit hours. This loop prioritizes open
visibility.

Can i repeat the loop?

Absolutely. Loop it as many times as you like to build distance gradually.
